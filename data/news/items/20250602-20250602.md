    香港八和會館自成立七十餘年以來，始終致力於弘揚傳統粵劇藝術及維護同業權益。

    本次活動將由龍貫天主席聯同阮兆輝、吳仟峰、新劍郎、南鳳、高麗、陳鴻進、梁兆明等多位資深及新進演員共同演出，旨在為觀眾提供高水準的表演，同時為本會長期以來維護業界利益與推廣粵劇文化的工作籌募經費。


    節目時長：3小時\

    Fundraising Performance: The Chinese Artists Association of Hong Kong “Pear Garden Convergence- A Tribute to Cantonese Opera” 2025

    Dates: July 22 & 23, 2025 (Tuesday & Wednesday)

    Time: 7:15 PM – 11:25 PM

    Venue: Grand Theatre, Hong Kong Cultural Centre

    Ticketing Website: https://www.urbtix.hk/

    Tickets are now available on URBTIX starting June 2.


    For over 70 years since its establishment, the Hong Kong Eight Harmony Association has been dedicated to promoting traditional Cantonese opera and safeguarding the rights of industry practitioners.


    The Association is proud to present the fundraising performance "Harmony in Cantonese Opera" on July 22 & 23, 2025, at the Hong Kong Cultural Centre. The program will feature the classic ceremonial piece "The Grand Alliance of the Six States" (六國大封相) as well as a selection of exquisite opera excerpts.


    This event will be led by Chairman Lung Koon-tin, alongside esteemed performers including Yuen Siu-fai, Ng Chin-fung, San Kim-long, Nam Fung, Ko Lai, Chan Hung-chun, Leung Siu-ming, and other veteran and emerging artists. The performance aims to deliver top-tier entertainment while raising funds for the Association’s ongoing efforts to uphold industry interests and promote Cantonese opera culture.


    Duration: 3 hours

    Age Restriction: Children under 6 will not be admitted.
